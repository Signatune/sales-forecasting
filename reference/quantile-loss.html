Quantile / pinball loss

Compressed cheat-sheet. For the walkthrough see Lesson 01.

Per-day loss (this repo’s convention)

diff = actual − forecast
actual ≥ forecast → loss = level × diff  (under-forecast)
actual < forecast → loss = (1 − level) × (−diff)  (over-forecast)

Headline score = mean of daily losses. Lower is better.

At level = 0.95

  • Under-forecast weight = 0.95; over-forecast weight = 0.05.
  • Asymmetry = 0.95 / 0.05 = 19× (a Stockout costs 19× an equal leftover).
  • Units are the same as the data — bagels of loss per day, roughly.

Conventions & gotchas

  • Factor of 2: FPP3 uses 2p/2(1−p) = 2× these numbers. Ranking-invariant.
  • What’s scored: the P95 buffered quantity, not the raw point forecast (p95_buffer, model_comparison.py:304).
  • At level = 0.5 pinball reduces to ½·MAE (symmetric).
  • Coverage is the companion: share of days quantity ≥ actual — realised Service Level.
